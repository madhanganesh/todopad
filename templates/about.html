Meet todopad — your focused productivity companion built to capture, organize, and complete your tasks. With AI-assisted tagging and simple workflows, staying on top of your to-dos has never been easier.

"What gets measured is what gets improved"

Intelligent Tagging

Tasks are automatically categorized with smart tags based on content — no manual input required. This helps you quickly filter, group, and track related items effortlessly.

Create Insights

Create insights based on tags that reveal trends in how your effort is being spent. This enables you to understand where your time goes and helps you optimize your actions to align with your goals.

Effortless Editing

Make changes on the fly — whether it’s updating a due date, marking a task complete, or adding notes. Every action is just a click away.

Designed for Mobile

Stay productive wherever you are. todopad is fully responsive and optimized for small screens — no app install needed.
